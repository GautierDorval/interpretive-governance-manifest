Interpretive governance

This domain hosts a machine-first governance manifest intended to reduce interpretive drift by enforcing explicit separation between observed facts, derived values, inferred hypotheses, and unknown fields.

Canonical files

  • interpretive-governance.manifest.json (canonical manifest)
  • .well-known/interpretive-governance.json (stable entrypoint)
  • schemas/manifest.schema.json (JSON Schema)
  • versions/ (version snapshots)
