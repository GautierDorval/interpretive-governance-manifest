- `causal-context-map.schema.json`

- `false-neighbor-behavioral-testset.schema.json`
- `proximity-causality-protocol.schema.json`

## Experimental measurement schemas

- `model-panel-declaration.schema.json`
- `judge-panel-declaration.schema.json`
- `false-neighbor-behavioral-run.schema.json`
- `proximity-causality-evaluation-run.schema.json`

The measurement schemas define report shapes only. They do not publish measured results and must not be represented as proof without populated evidence records.
