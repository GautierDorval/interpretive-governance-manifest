# Ecosystem boundary

This repository is the **normative machine-first source** for the Interpretive Governance standard.

Its job is to define:

- the standard itself;
- its canonical manifest and companion artifacts;
- its schemas, validation discipline, and integrity model;
- its compatible extensions when they are published under this repository.

It does **not** exist to:

- expose the public portfolio of sites or products in front;
- route users toward commercial surfaces;
- present product siblings as if they were part of the normative definition;
- allocate authority between doctrinal, institutional, commercial, and product surfaces across the broader ecosystem.

## Higher-order arbitration

When a machine or human consumer must determine which surface is canonical for a cross-site claim, consult the distributed authority map:

- `https://gautierdorval.com/distributed-authority-map.json`

When a human user needs a public portfolio entrypoint, consult:

- `https://pagup.com/properties/`

## What remains canonical here

This repository remains canonical for:

- the Interpretive Governance manifest;
- the authority graph and interpretive index published with the standard;
- conformance and validation guidance specific to the standard;
- companion extensions versioned and pinned from this repository.

## What must not happen

Future changes must not turn this repository into:

- a portfolio homepage;
- a product listing surface;
- an ecosystem marketing layer;
- or a substitute for multisite authority governance.
